# Superstore Sales Performance Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes sales performance using the Superstore dataset by developing an interactive Power BI dashboard. The dashboard enables business stakeholders to monitor overall sales performance, evaluate product profitability, and understand customer purchasing behavior through clear, interactive visualizations.

---

## Business Problem

Business leaders often struggle to quickly identify sales trends, profitable products, and high-value customer segments when working with large amounts of raw sales data.

This dashboard transforms transactional sales data into meaningful insights that support informed, data-driven business decisions.

---

## Objectives

- Monitor overall business performance
- Analyze sales across product categories
- Evaluate product profitability
- Identify top-performing customers
- Compare customer segments
- Present findings through interactive dashboards

---

## Tools Used

- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX
- Microsoft Excel

---

## Dashboard Pages

### Executive Dashboard

Provides an overview of:

- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Total Orders
- Average Discount
- Sales by Category
- Profit by Region

### Product Analysis

Analyzes:

- Top 10 Products by Sales
- Top 10 Products by Profit
- Bottom 10 Products by Profit
- Regional filtering using a slicer

### Customer Analysis

Examines:

- Sales by Segment
- Profit by Segment
- Top 10 Customers by Sales
